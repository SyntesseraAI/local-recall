The Orama vector store uses cosine distance for similarity scoring with results ranging from 0.0 (no match) to 1.0 (identical). Scores are rounded to 2 decimal places. When scores are equal, results use recency tie-breaking based on the `occurred_at` field to rank newer memories first.
