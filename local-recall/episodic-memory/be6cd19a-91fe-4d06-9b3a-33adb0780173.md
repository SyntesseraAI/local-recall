The SessionStart hook in `src/hooks/session-start.ts` performs a **full reload** of memories on each session, not a delta. It creates a fresh `MemoryManager` instance, loads all memories, sorts by `occurred_at` descending, and injects the 5 most recent memories into context. No incremental/delta loading mechanism exists.
