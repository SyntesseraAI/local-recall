Search results use cosine distance similarity scoring with a range of 0.0 (no match) to 1.0 (identical). Scores are rounded to 2 decimal places. Results are sorted by score descending. When scores are equal (tie), more recent memories (based on `occurred_at` timestamp) are ranked first as a tie-breaker.
