The BGE-small-en-v1.5 embedding model (~133MB) is automatically downloaded on first use and cached in local_cache/. Initial startup may take 30-60 seconds for the download. Subsequent runs load from cache. If the cache becomes corrupted, it can be manually cleared and will re-download on next run.
